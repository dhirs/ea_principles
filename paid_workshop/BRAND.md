# Brand

- **Primary brand colour: `#7D2FDB`** (purple). Use this as the dominant colour in all slides/visuals — it replaces the previous navy (`#1E2761`).
- Supporting tints (derived from the brand purple):
  - Gradient end (header): `#9B57E8`
  - Light accent / on-dark text (was ice blue): `#E7D8FB`
  - Divider on dark cards: `#6B3DB0`
- Accent (focus / highlight): gold `#E8B23A`
- Neutrals: slate text `#3A445C`, grey `#64748B`, light card `#F4F6FB`, border `#E1E6F2`.
- Alert/negative: `#C0392B`.
